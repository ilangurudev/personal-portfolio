Late evening in Miami's vibrant streets, where the neon glow creates a cinematic atmosphere. The interplay of artificial light and natural shadows reveals the city's dual nature—both welcoming and mysterious.

This shot captures a fleeting moment of urban solitude. I was drawn to how the warm light contrasted with the cool evening air, creating layers of depth that tell multiple stories at once.

The 24mm focal length allowed me to embrace the environment while maintaining the intimate feel of a candid street scene.
